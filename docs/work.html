What I do

My background is in data analytics and AI engineering, applied to commercial problems. Day to day, that means automating and optimising workflows across commercial planning, performance reporting, customer value management (CVM), sales operations, and pricing — the kind of work that's traditionally manual, spreadsheet- heavy, and slow, and turning it into something faster and more reliable.

I'm interested in where AI automation genuinely removes friction from a commercial team's day, rather than adding another dashboard nobody opens.
