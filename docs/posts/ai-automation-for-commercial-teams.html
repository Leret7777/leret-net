What I Actually Do: AI Automation for Commercial Teams

I'm a Commercial Intelligence Specialist — I sit at the intersection of data analytics and AI automation, building tools and workflows that make commercial functions faster and less manual. Day to day that means commercial planning, performance reporting, customer value management (CVM), sales operations, and pricing — the kind of work that's traditionally spreadsheet-heavy and slow, turned into something faster and more reliable.

I'm most interested in where AI automation genuinely removes friction from a commercial team's day, rather than adding another dashboard nobody opens.
